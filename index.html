55 Years of Right Care, Right Here

Capitol Medical Center, Inc. (CMCI) has been a beacon of medical excellence in the Philippines for more than five decades. Accredited as a Center for Excellence by the Philippine Health Insurance Corp (PHIC), CMCI strives to make a difference in the lives of patients and the community through consistently adhering to the “Right Care, Right Here” philosophy of service — its unique brand of service transcending from the 1970s to the present.
